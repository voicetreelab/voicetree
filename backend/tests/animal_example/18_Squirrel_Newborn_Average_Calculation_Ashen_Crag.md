### Calculates the average newborn squirrel rate in Ashen Crag based on parrot and eagle newborn rates.

The average number of newborn children per adult squirrel in Ashen Crag equals the difference between the average number of newborn children per adult parrot in Bundle Ranch and the average number of newborn children per adult eagle in Hamilton Farm.

### Calculates the adult beaver count in Ashen Crag based on parrot counts and newborn averages.

The number of adult beaver in Ashen Crag equals the sum of the number of adult parrot in Bundle Ranch and the average number of newborn children per adult parrot in Jefferson Circus.

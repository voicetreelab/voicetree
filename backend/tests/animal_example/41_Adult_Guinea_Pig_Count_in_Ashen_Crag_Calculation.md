### Calculates the adult guinea pig count in Ashen Crag as a sum of parrot newborn averages from Hamilton Farm and Bundle Ranch.

The number of adult guinea pig in Ashen Crag equals the sum of the average number of newborn children per adult parrot in Hamilton Farm and the average number of newborn children per adult parrot in Bundle Ranch.

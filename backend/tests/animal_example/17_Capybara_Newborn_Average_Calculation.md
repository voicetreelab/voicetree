### Calculates the average newborn per capybara in Ashen Crag based on parrot and blue jay newborn averages.

The average number of newborn children per adult capybara in Ashen Crag equals the sum of the number of adult parrot in Mayer Aquarium and the average number of newborn children per adult blue jay in Mayer Aquarium.

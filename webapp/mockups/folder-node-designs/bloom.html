typography

Display: Fraunces variable italic, opsz 144, soft 100, wonk 1 — gives the inscription a hand-drawn lilt. Body: Cormorant Garamond, light. Labels: Geist Mono in tight all-caps to keep the technical edge.

palette

Dyed paper warmth — cream #F5ECDC, terracotta #C25A3A, sage #7E8E5E, gold #C89859, oxblood ink #4A1C18. Children are tinted by primitive class; the folder membrane is a wash, never a fill.

motion

The collapsed bud breathes at ~5s per cycle, ±1.2% scale. Expanding plays a 320ms ease-out where the membrane unfolds and members "float in" with 50ms stagger. Edges grow last, with a 180ms drift.

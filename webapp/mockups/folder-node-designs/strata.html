A folder is a labeled enclosure in the field. Edges meet the enclosure at its border, never the children inside. Collapsed folders publish only their edge interface and a fanned count; expanded folders show every member as a stamped plate within a hatched ground. The visual language borrows from architectural plates: corner ticks, double rules, tabs, blueprint cyan as the single living color.

typography

Display: Major Mono Display, all-lowercase, occasional inverted tab pulled from technical labels. Body: IBM Plex Mono 400/500. Letterforms inherit the drafting plate's rigor.

palette

Paper #ECE2CD, ink #141414, blueprint #1F5D7A as the only chromatic move. Warning red #B8421A reserved for spec-status nodes. No gradients on nodes themselves.

motion

Collapse animation = camera dolly: enclosure contracts to a stamped plate, member plates fold inward and become the fanned right-edge count. No bounce. ~220ms ease-out.
